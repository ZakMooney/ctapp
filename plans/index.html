FAQs

Are there any limits on the free plan?

The free plan comes with a splash page view limit, and is missing advanced features.

What are the differences between the plans?

Features and AP cost. The main differences between the paid plans are the white label and advanced analytics features.

What is a splash page view?

Every time a guest user tries to connect to a Captive Portal network they will see a splash page. We count how many hits there are to your splash page per month.

What is QoS?

QoS prioritises the jobs that are sent to your boxes. A job might be when you change your SSID or reboot your boxes.

If load is high, we'll processes priority jobs faster.

What are Integrations?

Connect your other WebApps with Cucumber. Eg. automatically sync your guests with your Salesforce account or send an alert to your Slack account if a box goes offline.

What are Zones?

Zones allow you break up your networks into logical groups within their location. Each zone can contain any number of boxes and SSIDs.

MS (Distribution) UK Ltd.

Not only are MS Dist official distributors for Ubiquiti, MikroTik, and Mimosa (amongst others), they're also a great group of people who care about what they do and who they're doing it for. The MS Dist support team are all qualified with the latest certifications from Ubiquiti and MikroTik and able to help you with all your networking needs - from basic set-ups to more complex installations!

You can also get in touch if you're looking to train on one of Ubiquiti's or MikroTik's certified courses. If you just want to pick up some networking basics MS Dist also run their own TCP/IP in day training course.

Ruckus Virtual SmartZone Captive Portal Integration

Create a captive portal for your Ruckus network quickly and easily.

Cucumber Tony integrates with the Ruckus vSZ seamlessly. Through the Cucumber dashboard you can design splash pages and monitor your Ruckus APs.

Branding

Create a splash page to match yours or your client's brand. The splash pages are easy to set up and require no coding or hosting.

Add colour ways, logos, copy and images. Then choose a login method to suit your network - social logins, registration form, vouchers, click through or paid access through Paypal are all available.

Access Points Online

Once your Ruckus sVZ is integrated with Cucumber you can manage certain features using the simple dashboard.

You'll see a live status feed of the access points on your network, and if one goes offline you'll get an alert.

Clients Connected

The dashboard gives you a real-time stream of people connected so you can see how much people are downloading instantly.

As well as the live feed you can search through reports instantly.

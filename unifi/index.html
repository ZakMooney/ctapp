Cucumber Your APs

Flash your UniFi APs with Cucumber

Follow our simple guides and get your APs running our custom firmware. If you're unhappy, you can always revert back to the UniFi firmware.

Add them to the Dashboard

Add your newly flashed UniFi APs into your dashboard with our handy auto-scan tool, or add them directly using their MAC address.

Multi-Vendor

You're not tied down to just using UniFi hardware, manage APs from multiple vendors within the same location. A full list of our supported hardware can be found here

Configure Your Networks

Create your Locations

Manage all of your locations from one dashboard. You can even break the management down even further by using zones in your locations.

Setup your Networks

Create and configure the networks you need. Add secure private networks, and splash enabled public networks, the choice is yours.

Install your UniFi APs

Ship your UniFi APs to their site and connect them up. Simple as that.

The Cucumber dashboard will do all the work and send all of your settings down to your AP.

Just sit back and watch them pop online in your dashboard.

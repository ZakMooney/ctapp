Insights and analytics for physical locations

Measure footfall and understand customer behaviour using your WiFi network.

The Cucumber platforms gives you accurate data on how people engage with your store. Helping you improve performance and measure success.

Accurate footfall

The Cucumber platform tracks visitors using WiFi signal from mobile devices. Visitors do not need to connect to the WiFi to be counted.

This gives you accurate footfall and dwell time figures across your stores in real-time.

Measure the impact of events

Compare performance across time in line with your marketing initiatives and events.

Do window displays improve footfall? Does a new floor layout increase the average dwell time in store?

Map user paths

Cucumber Tony maps routes people walked and areas they gathered to give you the most popular areas in your store. This image is of the walked paths around a town centre.

Use this to set rent rates, manage traffic and place advertising.

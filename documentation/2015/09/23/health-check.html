Running the Health Check

Sep 23, 2015

To run the Health Check, follow these steps:

1. Head into your location in Cucumber Tony and select Boxes on the left hand sidebar.

2. Select the box that you want to run the check on.

3. Scroll down under the Tools column and click on the Checkup button.

4. Once you have run it, you will get a status message below the button which you can click on to see a summary of the health check.

What is it?

The Health Check runs a selection of checks on your box to see if there are any potential issues. The things it checks are as follows:

  • Internet Check - This checks if the box has a working internet connection
  • DNS Check - This checks if the box can reach a DNS server
  • MTU Check - This compares the MTU of the box with the MTU used on your network
  • Connected to Cucumber - This checks if the box can reach the Cucumber Tony servers
  • Splash Pages - This checks if there are splash pages running on your network
  • In Sync - This checks if your box's configuration matches up to what it should be in Cucumber Tony

Common issues

Your box MTU is too high. Reduce this or increase on your modem.

To change the MTU settings on the box just follow these steps:

  • Head into Boxes on the left hand sidebar
  • Click on the box that you want to make changes on
  • Click on Settings across the top of the page
  • Scroll down for a section called Connectivity Settings
  • You will find a field for changing your MTU here

We recommend dropping your MTU on the box by 8 from that of your network.

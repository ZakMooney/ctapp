Manage OpenWrt Devices from the Cloud

A new and easy way to manage and monitor your OpenWrt devices.

It was getting too much managing networks using spreadsheets, remote desktop and the CLI. We wanted a way to configure and manage our OpenWrt devices over any browser with a simple and intuitive interface.

So we went and made it.

We created a package that goes hand in hand with OpenWrt to allow you to manage your devices from the cloud. Along with a dashboard where you can add your devices and configure them.

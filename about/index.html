Meet The Team

Cucumber is a fast growing, London based start-up that has so far bootstrapped our way to building an exceptional Wi-Fi device management platform. In deferring funding, we have carefully utilised every resource, ensuring Cucumber is fully operational even when it shouldn’t.

We’re a small young team of 5 who are committed to making Cucumber the only management platform you’ll ever need for Wi-Fi devices.

Cucumber Network Features

Create and configure your networks and guest access with these features.

Multiple Guest & Private Networks

Create multiple networks, and offer a separate quick and easy way for your guests to connect to your WiFi network.

Time of Day SSIDs

Set up your SSIDs to only broadcast during the time that you want.

Content Filtering

Choose from our levels of content filtering to secure your network, meet your legal requirements and prevent your WiFi users from abusing your WiFi network.

Integrate Advanced Content Filtering

If you have bespoke requirements from your content filtering, you can integrate CT to use the content filtering service of your choice.

Firewall Settings

Establish your rules to restrict or allow specific types of inbound and outbound traffic, keeping your network more secure.

Port Forwarding

Configure your own port forwarding rules to get your traffic directed to where you need it.

Proxy Settings

Direct your traffic through a proxy server easily by simply entering the details into your Cucumber dashboard.

User & Device Blocking

People tend to abuse free WiFi networks. Keep everyone happy by sharing the WiFi love equally. If anyone is overdoing downloading files, log them out with one quick function.

CT WiFi Management Features

Manage and monitor your devices from the cloud with these features.

Cloud Manage

Get rid of that controller and ditch that extra software.

Manage your devices effortlessly from anywhere through the cloud.

Admins

Give users access to your CT WiFi dashboard by assigning them to permission groups to suit their roles.

Bring in your colleague to help manage your dashboard, give a staff member access to generate batches of vouchers, or invite your client in to view their location reports.

Keep your dashboard secure by only giving access to what they need.

Remote Health Checks

Run a health check on any of your devices without being on location to get a breakdown of possible issues.

Automated Firmware Upgrades

Keep your devices running the latest and greatest version of CT WiFi to ensure that they have the latest firmware features and fixes, all without having to worry to update them all yourself.

Locations

Organise your devices based on locations.

Configure custom settings and create splash pages that are unique from your other locations.

Zones

Simplify device management and add your devices into zones to manage groups of devices within a location.

Want to configure different network/access settings for your outdoor area and indoor areas? Just put them into seperate zones.

Logs

Access in-depth logs from your devices, as well as information from your dashboard.

Alerts

Get notified of your device's status with alerts to let you know when it drops offline, comes back online and much more.

History

View a history of changes made within your CT WiFi dashboard.

Events

You can set events to automatically run on your devices. Events are rules you can create for your device so that at set intervals your Wi-Fi device carries out a specific job.

Triggers

Triggers run automatically in the background. They allow you to send a message whenever an event occurs, such as sending a message to Slack when a box goes offline.

Custom Scripts

Send custom scripts to your devices from your CT WiFi dashboard.

SSH Access

SSH into your device with all the details needed displayed on your device's page in your dashboard.

Self Hosted Syslog

Set up your devices to send their logs to your own syslog server.

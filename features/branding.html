CT WiFi Branding Features

Customise your CT WiFi experience. Add your colours, logo, theme.

White Label Dashboard

Get rid of the CT WiFi branding and personalise your dashboard theme to suit yourself.

Custom Dashboard URL

Customise your dashboard url to include your own brand.

CName

If the custom URL isn't enough, you can white lable your dashboard by setting up your CName. Access your CT WiFi dashboard using your own domain.

White Label Emails

You can brand your email alerts and report summary with CT WiFi, as well as set them up so they are sent from an address of your choice.

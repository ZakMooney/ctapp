Cucumber Architecture

The Cucumber platform is built within Google Compute Engine (GCE). The service is formed of over 2 dozen micro-services that provide high-availability, redundancy and performance.

The network is distributed globally, with data centres in the EU, US and Asia.

We utilise a number of Google's tools to boost performance, including BigQuery for data warehousing and PUBSUB for high-performance queues.

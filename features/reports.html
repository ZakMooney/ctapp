Cucumber Reporting Features

View in-depth reports from your devices and networks.

Weekly Report Summary

Have a summary report emailed to you every week, including guest splash usage and other details.

Fine Granularity Reports

Refine your reports down to the small or large scale that you need.

CSV Download

Export your reports and captured data as CSV files for easy download and maximum compatibility.

Google Analytics Integration

Integrate Google analytics with your splash pages to help track their traffic.

Live Clients

Get a live list of clients connected to your networks.

Splash Page Views

Up to date count on how many views your splash page is getting.

Usage Reports

Detailed reports on your client's usage while connected to your network.

Top Access Points

View a breakdown of your most used devices.

Top Locations

See which of your locations are getting the most use, along with a breakdown of their stats.

Top Users

Find out which users keep coming back to make the most of your network.

Social Media Profiling

Capture social media information when your guests sign in using social access.

Returning Users vs Unique Users

Easily view which percentage of your users are returning and which are new users.

Average Connection Time

Quickly and easily see what the average connection times are for your locations.

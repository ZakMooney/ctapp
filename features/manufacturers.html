Cloud Management For Your Hardware

Cucumber Tony provides an intuitive cloud platform that allows businesses to manage their WiFi networks from a single dashboard.

Consumers now expect cloud management. Gone are the days of using 192.168.0.1. Cucumber Tony gives you a cost effective way to deploy your cloud solution. Stay ahead of the competition and bring your hardware to market faster.

The Dashboard

Cucumber Tony is the first WiFi platform with a fully featured public RESTful API. Build your own applications on top, or offer this as an extra service to your clients.

Unlike typical vendor deployment which adhere to 3-6 month release cycles, Cucumber Tony releases fixes and upgrades daily. We treat WiFi as software, increasing the reliability whilst reducing release times.

Setting up and shipping your devices is a breeze. You can provision custom configs per customer or groups of customers.

Upload your own firmware to the management platform and deploy it automatically to your customers.

The Data

The platform provides everything you need to monitor and manage your networks. Data is collated and presented in real-time, reflecting the full performance of the networks. Never seen before outside the enterprise, Cucumber Tony is changing the way people use and set up networks.

Simple, beautiful charts and graphs describe your networks visually enabling you to quickly diagnose problems and forecast network requirements.

The Beautiful and Customisable Interface

Cucumber Tony is yours to enjoy, brand, and redesign. We provide all the tools to get started. The entire front-end can be completely re-branded to match yours. The simple, modular design lets you add or remove customer components.

The interface can be hosted by Cucumber Tony or as part of your own deploy cycle.

How it Works

The system is defined by three parts:

  1. The front-end interface - the dashboard
  2. The back-end platform that controls the devices
  3. The daemon that runs on the devices providing secure communication

The process is simple. We provide a simple daemon that you install on your firmware. This communicates with Cucumber Tony, providing a bi-directional, secure way to communicate with your access points.

Access points provision automatically and reconfigure themselves directly.

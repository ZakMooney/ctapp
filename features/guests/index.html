Cucumber Guest Access Features

Manage and monitor your devices from the cloud with these features.

Fully Customisable Splash Page

The splash pages are unbranded and fully responsive. Choose layouts, colours, fonts and compose your own message using the template designer.

If you can code, use your own CSS. Or, if you can really code, use the API and build your own!

Master Splash Page

Create and design a master splash page, and use it as a template for all of your new ones rather than starting from scratch each time.

Paid Access

Monetise your guest access and sell vouchers directly from your splash page. Charge what you want for the access you want to provide.

Tiered Login

Configure tiered logins and give your guests a secondary login once their first has expired.

Clickthrough Login

Keep your guest access simple and clean, allow them to log in at the tap of a button.

Password Login

Restrict access through your splash page by setting a password, giving it to only those who you want to log in.

Social Login

Have your guests sign in using their social media accounts such as Facebook.

Voucher/Coupon Login

Hand out vouchers to your guests to give them an access type specific to the username and password they use.

Quick Codes

Quick codes are a quick and easy way to offer temporary internet access. Choose a password and set some limits, then give them to your guest.

Custom Registration Access

Create your a registration page to capture the guest information you want, with custom fields.

External RADIUS Support

Use your own RADIUS server to authenticate your guest logins.

External Splash Page Support

Integrate your own captive portal provider with our dashboard.

3rd Party Integration Support

We support captive portal management for devices without installing our firmware on them, from Meraki to DDWRT.

Time of Day Splash Pages

Configure your splash pages to only allow logins during a specified time of day to keep control on network use.

Timed Usage Restrictions

Restrict your splash page guests based on how long they can spend online.

Data Usage Restrictions

Restrict your splash page guests based on how much data they download.

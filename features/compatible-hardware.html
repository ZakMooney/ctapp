There are three different ways to integrate with Cucumber.

The whole shebang. Install the OpenWRT based firmware onto a compatible device. Enterprise-grade management features on everyday devices. Full control and visibility of your devices from the cloud. Hell yeah.

Cucumber Firmware

Fully manage your access points

Install our OpenWRT based firmware onto your access points and manage them from the Cucumber dashboard.

You get access to a cloud portal which includes management, networking, reporting, guest access and analytics modules.

We support a wide range of devices including the devices below.

API Integration

Monitor your access points and stats

Hook into these manufacturer's APIs so you can integrate it with Cucumber Tony. Get realtime network stats, just like your Cucumber APs.

Configure your splash pages, view your wireless clients, and even view the online status of your APs all within the dashboard.

Splash Page Integration

Configure your guest access

You can also use Cucumber Tony's guest WiFi features without installing our OpenWRT based firmware.

Just adjust your radius and captive portal settings and you can use our Splash Pages with these bits of kit and more.

There are three different ways to integrate with Cucumber.

By installing the firmware onto a compatible device, you gain access to countless management and networking features that allow you to control and monitor your device from the cloud, along with reporting and guest access options.

We also offer integration via our API which allows you to monitor your devices, while still getting access to the reports and guest access options.

Finally, you can integate solely with our captive portal. Create and design your splash page, and configure the guest access options to suit your needs.

Cucumber Firmware

Install our firmware onto your access points and manage them from the Cucumber dashboard.

You get access to a cloud portal which includes management, networking, reporting, guest access and analytics modules.

API Integration

We are able to hook into their API so you can integrate it with our splash pages, view your wireless clients, and even view the online status of your APs all within the Cucumber dashboard.

Splash Page Integration

You can also use Cucumber Tony's guest WiFi features without installing our firmware.

Just adjust your radius and captive portal settings and you can use our Splash Pages with these bits of kit and more.

WiFi Workshop for Aspiring WiFi Professionals

Register your interest now and get notified when dates are announced

CT WiFi is bringing you an introductory course into WiFi with Nigel. As a well-respected UK wireless professional and a certified wireless network expert (CWNE), Nigel has achieved CWNA, CWSP, CWAP & CWDP certifications, and has a proven track record of experience & contribution to the industry.

Are you:

  • An IT admin that knows the basics of running a network
  • A systems engineer that wants to expand into WiFi networking
  • Or someone looking to start a career in WiFi networking

Then this is the workshop for you. You'll be learning how to:

  • Choose the right kit for the right venue
  • Identify WiFi bands and channels
  • Identify and resolve performance issues
  • Understand WiFi components and technology
  • Understand Radio Frequency theory
  • Understand wireless infrastructure
  • Troubleshoot problems with WiFi

This course will be focusing on practicals and use cases so you can apply theory to practice. You’ll have an opportunity to network with like-minded WiFi enthusiasts. Free refreshments and lunch included too.
